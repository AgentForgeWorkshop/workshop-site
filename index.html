Why this workshop

Agentic AI is moving fast — but the conferences are built for AI researchers, not the scientists who could benefit most. AgentForge closes that gap: practical upskilling for domain scientists who haven't yet brought agents into their daily research.

01

From scratch & on platforms

Understand the architecture of an agent — then build one from the ground up and on existing platforms like Claude Code and Antigravity.

02

Private & local

Deploy secure, privacy-preserving agents you control — fit for sensitive scientific data and proprietary research.

03

Tailored to your science

Literature review, querying specialised databases, running custom analysis scripts — agents shaped around your real workflows.

The curriculum

Five tutorials, building toward an autonomous research assistant.

  • T1From Chatbot to AgentThe ReAct framework — build a literature-scout agent that searches arXiv.→
  • T2Agentic Memory & RAGGive agents domain knowledge without hallucinating facts.→
  • T3MCP & SkillsTool-calling, the Model Context Protocol, file manipulation, ToolUniverse.→
  • T4Multi-Agent OrchestrationAgents that communicate, debate, and delegate across roles.→
  • T5Autonomous AgentsAn end-to-end assistant — and always-on frameworks like OpenClaw.→

Then: the hackathon

Two days. Your data. A working agent.

Form groups of 3–5 by discipline, bring your own datasets and bottlenecks, and ship a custom agent — closing with a five-minute live demo and an award.
