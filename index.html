About the Workshop

What is an AI Agent? An AI agent goes beyond a standard chatbot. It is a system that uses an AI model to pursue a goal, make decisions, plan steps, and use external tools or data sources — carrying out multi-step tasks with varying levels of human oversight.

Why AI Agents as Scientific Research Assistants? Modern research generates more data and literature than any individual can keep up with. AI agents can automate repetitive tasks, analyze datasets, synthesize literature, and connect knowledge across disciplines — while human scientists provide the judgment, verification, and context that keep research accurate and meaningful.

What You Will Learn and Do

  • Understand the core architecture of AI agents and their applications in scientific research
  • Explore core agent components, including memory, tools, skills, and multi-agent systems
  • Build prototype AI research assistants using coding and low-code platforms
  • Develop evaluation criteria to assess the accuracy and trustworthiness of their agents
  • Collaborate in a hackathon to solve domain-specific research challenges
